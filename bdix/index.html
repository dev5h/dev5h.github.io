BDIX

BDIX is the first Internet Exchange Point (IXP) of Bangladesh. It was established to provide physical interconnection for its members to exchange and route local Internet traffic locally through it. Since 2014, BDIX has maintained leadership of the fast-developing Internet industry of Bangladesh by providing high quality services that provided fast, cost-effective and efficient Internet connections for its members and their customers. BDIX is a not-for-profit venture of the Sustainable Development Networking Foundation (SDNF) Bangladesh. So far more than 110 Organisations of different types (ISPs, Mobile Operators, Content Providers) are peering through BDIX.

Why BDIX Finder?

As the internet is growing everyday, new ISPs are spreading across the country. As new sites are being connected to the BDIX network, some are being deprecated. As an individual person it is very hard to keep track of all the sites. Also whenever you switch your network your working servers are also changing. This ap is there to ensure you never get deprecated of the BDIX network.

How it works?

Well, the working process is very simple. We already have a list of bunch of top BDIX servers which are updated periodically. All we do is we try to connect each of the servers from your network through the app and keep a track of the response of the connection requests. After a successful scan we finally show you a list of your working servers through a beautiful UI.
